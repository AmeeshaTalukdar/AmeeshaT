My Favourite Books with Reviews

  1. Book: "Deception Point" by Dan Brown

    Summary: A gripping techno-thriller about a NASA discovery that could change the future of the planet. As the characters discover evidence of alien life, they are thrust into a dangerous conspiracy. A blend of scientific intrigue and thrilling action.

  2. Book: "The Alchemist" by Paulo Coelho

    Summary: A philosophical novel about a young shepherd named Santiago who embarks on a journey to find a treasure. Along the way, he learns valuable life lessons about destiny, self-discovery, and following his dreams.

  3. Book: "Murder on the Orient Express" by Agatha Christie

    Summary: A famous detective, Hercule Poirot, investigates a murder on the luxurious train, the Orient Express. The book is filled with suspense and clever twists as Poirot uncovers the complex motivations behind the crime.
